Student Accommodation Guide
Finding the Right Accommodation:
  • Consider proximity to campus and transportation
  • Check safety features and security measures
  • Review included amenities (WiFi, laundry, etc.)
  • Understand the rental agreement terms
  • Set a realistic budget including utilities
Application Tips:
  • Apply early (recommended 3-4 months before semester)
  • Prepare necessary documents in advance
  • Read reviews from current/former residents
  • Schedule viewings before committing
